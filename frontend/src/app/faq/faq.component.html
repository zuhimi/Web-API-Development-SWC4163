How do I add a new car?
To add a new car, go to the "Add Car" page and fill in the required details in the form.
Can I update the information of an existing car?
Yes, you can update the information of an existing car on the "Update Car" page by providing the new details.
How do I remove a car from the system?
To remove a Car, go to the "View Cars" page, find the car you want to remove, and click the "Remove" button.
